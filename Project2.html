    • Problem Statement

    The Nairobi Waste Management Scheme was invented to assist residents arond Nairobi suburbs;Mathare, Lang’ata, Karen & Westlands with organized waste collection and disposal.A project aimed at helping individuals and communities in Mathare, Lang’ata, Karen, Westlands & surrounding suburbs with proper garbage and litter collection. Clean city. Healthy people. ♻️

    The target

  • Mama mboga
  • Bodaboda riders
  • Local residents

    • Why Them

  • Many struggle to make ends meet
  • They already know the neighborhoods well
  • They are trusted by local residentsThey can easily be organized into waste collection teams

    • Solution

  • Poor waste collection:Introduce scheduled waste collection.

    Use online pickup request systems.

    Increase number of collection trucks.

  • Improper waste disposal.

    Provide dustbins in public areas.

    Enforce strict waste disposal laws.

    Penalize illegal dumping.

  • Lack of proper waste segregation:Use color-coded bins (organic, plastic, paper, e-waste).

    Educate households on waste sorting.

    Enforce waste segregation across the surbubs.

  • Excessive Plastic Waste:Promote recycling programs.

    Ban single-use plastics.

    Encourage reusable bags and bottle.

    The government to intervene as individuals also normalize practising waste collection and disposal for a greener future environment.
